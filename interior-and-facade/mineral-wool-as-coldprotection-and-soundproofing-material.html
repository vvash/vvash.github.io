Mineral wool as coldprotection and soundproofing material

Mineral wool - a universal insulation, which, thanks to a fairly wide                             Assortment list, can be used for sanitation of building envelopes,                             Built from any material.

                        

Different in density mats and stone wool rolls allow to insulate walls, ceilings, floors                             On the ground and roof. It is only necessary to choose the type and density of the product correctly, then                             The result will be what you expect.

                        

When insulating a building, it is important to remember that there is no universal insulation. For each                             Element (roof, wall or foundation) requires a thermal insulation material with                             Specific properties.

                        

As for the facades, there are three options to insulate the walls: lay the insulation inside                             Walls (frame construction) or to warm the finished walls outside or indoors. we                             Have repeatedly written about the fact that inside the walls there is no need to insulate the walls, because in the end                             This will lead to very serious negative consequences, therefore this kind of insulation even                             We will not consider.

                        

It is more appropriate to place mineral wool outside the facade. You can do this with two                             Ways: in the first case, a heater                             Is embedded in a crate filled on the walls (this method is suitable mainly for                             Wooden houses), in the second - is attached to the wall with dowels and glue (for stone                             Buildings). The use of "wet" processes associated with                             Plastering facades, gave the name of a "wet" way of warming.

                        

The advantage of a "dry" method of warming walls is that the work can be done in any                             season. On the facade of the building make a crate of bar and fill the formed                             Space heater. From above all this construction is covered with windproof films,                             So that the cotton wool is not blown by the wind, and closed with finishing materials: siding, blockhouse,                             Imitation bars and the like.

                        

Warming of the facade of a stone house

                        

At the beginning of the work, the surface must be prepared: remove all extraneous or                             Ancillary items from the facade, communications or prepare them for sewing into the system                             Insulation. Of course, if this does not contradict SNiPam. To go against the building rules is not                             As a rule, their positions make it possible to protect themselves from future problems.

                        

The drain systems must also be removed and installed after the finish, while                             You need to be very careful not to damage them.

                        

When preparing the working surface, make sure that no excess                             Items: rusty fittings, nails and other black metal, eliminate the highs and the inflows                             Interstitial solution.

                        

The protruding pieces of reinforcement, metal mesh and other metals need to be extracted, because in                             In the process of temperature oscillations, water condensate forms, which, upon interaction with                             Metals causes corrosion. If you do not remove them, then all rust will come up, spoiling                             Appearance of the plaster, but it is not the most important thing - the corrosion processes inside the system                             Gradually reduce its effectiveness.

Remove mold and fungus, moss and other microflora inmates. It is necessary to remove the old                             Plaster, especially in those places where it barely holds.

                        

After you have prepared the site, select a small training area for yourself                             walls. It only seems that it is easy to produce works, in fact before does not interfere                             Practice.

                        

Next, we map the sags, that is, we split the working area into zones where                             Horizontal, vertical and diagonal sags are installed. As them you can                             Use a normal rope.

                        

The test is set as follows: drill one hole at the top of the wall                             (This will be a vertical sag), and the other parallel to the bottom, drive there the anchor dowel                             Of the corresponding diameter, set the distance from the wall to the thickness of the insulation + 1 cm of stock                             On the adhesive solution and tighten the cord. Similarly, spread the sags across the surface                             Walls, with a pitch of 80-90 cm.

                        

Similarly, make at least two horizontal sags, one below, the other                             On top of the center area, you can make a couple more diagonal sags.

                        

The sagging system is not just a waste of time, it will help to study the relief of the wall, since                             Almost always it turns out that the facade is not a flat plane at all, but a cluster of pits and                             Bumps.

                        

Take a tape measure and walk through each sling, taking measurements at least 50 cm, this                             Standard height of insulation sheet. More or less normal vibrations are placed in the                             Range ± 5-10 mm, but it happens, these figures reach up to +100. To such surprises it is necessary to be                             Ready.

                        

Having such a card, during the gluing of the heater will not have to be applied every time                             Level, you only need to set the first row exactly horizontally, and then navigate                             On slips, only from time to time to control the plane by a level or rule.                         

                        

The final stage of the preparatory work is priming the surface. It is more convenient to conduct this                             Procedure before the installation of sags. As a tool it is more convenient to use                             Brush-makrovitsu.

With regard to the choice of material for priming, in this case, one must proceed from the data itself                             Walls. It can be just a primer of deep penetration or having                             Antifungal additives.

                        

Getting Started

                        

We begin the work with the installation of the plinth, which will provide support for the first row and                             Adjusts the clearance between the heater and the wall.

                        

To set a profile, define a zero point using the water level. Then                             With a padded cord, mark the line along the entire length of the wall. Now take the glue for the mineral wool and                             Grid, run the grid 10 cm above the target line and fix it with glue. Then attach                             Plinth, use a perforator to make a hole and secure the profile                             Dowel.

                        

Put the first row

                        

Use only special glue, diluted exactly according to the instructions!

                        

The solution is applied to the entire surface of the slab, then smoothed out with a comb spatula.                             Apply glue in this way, rather than point. This will allow the plate to be normal                             Grab the surface of the wall and add to it the rigidity. Very carefully refer to                             Installation of the first row - twist here, then it will be very difficult to fix it.

                        

If the wall has significant unevenness, then it must be aligned even before gluing                             Thermal insulation boards, and the period between these processes should be at least a week.                             Aligning irregularities during the gluing of plates will lead to insufficient strength                             Adhesive bonding.

                        

Make sure that there are no slots between the slabs - these are cold bridges that your                             Efforts to "no". Minplita must perfectly touch each other. If still                             Screwed up, close the cracks with inserts gently undermine.

                        

Mounting of plates is performed with a seam ligation of not less than 100 mm, during assembly it is desirable                             Use predominantly one-piece slabs.

                        

Absence of bandaging of seams or mosaic from the remains of the plate entail an increased                             The probability of crack formation, and cracks on the facade are small, they are very fast                             Grow and "multiply" under the influence of humidity and temperature fluctuations.

                        

Try to mount the plates without any differences in height - sharp transitions in the thickness of the reinforcing                             Layer will cause the occurrence of cracks in the finish plaster layer.

                        

Fix the plate with dowels, preferably composite, with a glass fiber nail. If not                             Attach them with dowels, then because of the high weight of the mineral wool, the insulated façade will collapse or                             Will go crack.

                        

Reinforcement

                        

After the plates are secured with dowels, you can start reinforcing. This stage                             It is necessary to strengthen the construction of insulation and protect the minvat itself.

                        

First apply the first layer of glue on mineral wool, then in this glue is embedded                             Reinforcing alkali-resistant mesh (glass mesh) and on it is applied a second layer of glue. Grid                             Is applied to the facade with an overlap of 10 cm. In places where the grid does not find a grid,                             The plaster layer cracks after a couple of hours, respectively, will also look like                             Decorative plaster.

                        

After the reinforcing layer has dried, it is leveled by applying putty and                             Primed with special compounds.

                        

Finishing Coating

                        

The last layer in the insulation of the facade is the decorative layer - various types of decorative                             Plasters (lambs, bark beetles, etc.), as well as facade paints. Main purpose                             Finishing layer - protection from precipitation, ultraviolet, wind, various chemicals,                             Contained in the air and, of course, aesthetics. In order for moisture to evaporate from                             Insulation, the decorative layer must have sufficient vapor permeability.
