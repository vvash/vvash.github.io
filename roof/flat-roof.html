Types of flat roof

                        

Operated

                        

Already from its name it is clear that on it will be heavy objects, people                             etc. Operated flat roof is used to arrange on the roof terrace, winter                             Gardens, greenhouses, etc.

The peculiarity of such a roof is that under the waterproofing a rigid foundation is laid or                             A screed so that the roof can withstand loads, while maintaining integrity and not                             Pushed.

                        

"Green"

                        

It is a kind of exploited roof and can be created on the basis of traditional                             Or the inversion structure of the "pie". The peculiarity of such a roof is the cultivation of plants on it                             And the creation of special layers that allow these plants to normally exist and                             Develop without harming the hydro- and thermal insulation.

                        

Inversion

                        

This is an alternative constructive solution to the traditional flat roof. The difference is,                             That the layer of insulation is not located under the waterproofing carpet, but above it. This                             The design allows to protect a waterproof carpet from destructive influence                             UV rays, sudden temperature changes, freeze-thaw cycles, and                             Mechanical damage. All this increases the service life of such a roof in comparison with                             Traditional soft roof.

                        

The slope of the inversion roof is recommended to be arranged at an angle of 2.5-5%. If the surface                             It is covered with a layer of gravel, melt and rainwater through the gravel layer flows down through                             Filter material and flows down the carpet of waterproofing to specially equipped                             Drainage devices.

                        

Traditional

                        

Represents a "pie" consisting of a bearing plate with a vapor barrier applied to it, on                             Which is laid insulation. On the insulation is made a mortar with a razuglonkoj, and on                             This screed is applied waterproofing protective carpet from roll materials.

                        

What is the "sick" flat roof?

                        

If you decide to build a house with a flat roof or already live in a house with such a roof, you                             It is necessary to remember about possible basic defects that occur during its operation.

                        

The accumulation of moisture in local areas leads to the fact that when heated under waterproofing                             Carpets are created high-pressure pockets. They tear the roof in problem areas. For                             Bitumen roofs are the places of the joints of the canvas and the adjacency. One way to deal with this                             Defect is the use of materials that can distribute internal vapor pressure                             Water under the waterproofing carpet and relieve the excess pressure.

                        

The causes of defects can be different: the absence of shrinkage joints at the base                             Roofing; Large gaps between the plates and the sagging of the roofing carpet; The presence of moisture between                             The top layer of the roofing cloth and the lower one; Moisture and dust in the base of the roof, etc.

                        

If the roof is cracked, swollen or leaking, it is worth checking the condition                             Thermal insulation - it happens that it gets wet and needs replacing. Classical technology for                             The elimination of these defects is the complete disassembly of the roofing carpet, drying or replacement                             Insulation, repair of vapor barrier layer. If the replacement of the thermal insulation layer is not possible                             (Insufficient funds, do not allow it to make weather conditions, etc.), then, in                             In the extreme case, through it, with a certain step, flywalls, places of interface with                             The roof is sealed, and with time, the natural drying of the roof occurs.

                        

To avoid such defects and the cost of future repairs, experts today recommend                             Choose the installation of roofing covering the technology of "breathing roof."

                        

The term "breathable" roof means that a roofing carpet can pass in one direction a pair                             Water, removing them into the atmosphere from the thermal insulation layer.

For bitumen roofs this is achieved by using a special lower layer that has                             A branched network of air channels connected to each other. Thanks to these channels, pairs                             Water can be distributed over the entire surface of the roof, and to divert excess                             Pressure apply special aerial aerators - flywagks.

                        

Fluxplugs are also used in a membrane roof in the case if it consists of an elastomer, or                             When it is necessary to intensify the output of vapors when repairing old roofs.

                        

The process of ventilation allows you to extend the life of bitumen roofs at times. The main reason                             Aging "breathing" roofs in this case will be the impact of ultraviolet.

                        

If we are talking about bituminous materials, then although they are the most traditional, affordable and cheap,                             While they are also short-lived and not technologically advanced. Their quality can not be considered                             Sufficient for modern construction. Over time, the cost of roof repairs with                             Such a coating may exceed the cost of their original device.

                        

But the use of synthetic membrane materials at the present stage acquires all                             Great popularity because of their longer service life. They are resistant to                             UV-radiation, aggressive environments, mechanical and climatic loads, fireproof,                             Resistant to aging.

                        

The most common defects that arise when installing flat roofs are associated with the level                             Training roofer. This includes neprovar stitches, ignorance of standard knots,                             Violation of technology. As a rule, the weakest places are the seams themselves, the junctions of the                             Walls, parapets, pipes, stands, night tables, funnels. Such defects appear in the first                             Same half a year of operation.

Regarding the repair of flat roofs, it is difficult to find and get to the place of leakage, if                             The roof is filled with something. On the roofs to be exploited, the top layers must be removed to                             Get to the waterproofing. In polymeric roofs, there is rarely a fully glued                             System, so that sometimes there is a difficulty with the localization of the leakage source.

                        

Insulation features

                        

The base of the flat roof is a load-bearing slab, reinforced concrete, monolithic or from a profiled sheet.                             On top of it is a vapor barrier material, then a layer of thermal insulation.

                        

There is a one- and two-layer system of thermal insulation of a flat roof, the latter is used more often:                             The lower layer thickness of 70-200 mm takes on the basic thermal insulation function, the top layer                             Thickness of 30-50 mm is responsible mainly for the redistribution of mechanical stress and                             It is characterized by high compressive strength and density. This "specialization" of the layers                             "Pie" flat roof can significantly reduce its weight and, accordingly, the load on the                             Overlapping.

                        

Ventilation device

                        

Water vapor, which is constantly formed in the premises of the house, due to diffusion and convective                             The process rises upward and, by cooling, condenses in the under-roof space,                             Especially in the winter. Overabundant condensation leads to wet spots and mold                             On walls and ceiling, and excess moisture, accumulating in heat-insulating material, reduces it                             Properties.

                        

Roof fans (aerators), special pipes of different types                             Diameters of metal or plastic, covered from above with caps in the shape of umbrellas.

                        

The principle of the aerator is based on the difference in pressures caused by air currents. Him                             The design allows moisture vapor to freely flow out from under the roofing carpet, without damaging                             The latter and preventing the formation of bubbles and flaking of the roofing material.

                        

On flat roofs of a simple configuration, aerators are placed evenly over the entire area in                             The highest points of the roof plane in the places of joints of heat-insulating plates. Their                             Install better when installing the roof itself.

One of the main reasons for reducing the service life of the roofing is due to the formation                             Stagnant zones on its surface. In such places after precipitation are formed first                             Puddles, then life begins in them: the seeds of plants are brought in by the wind, moss begins to grow,                             Grass and even trees (in especially neglected cases). Roots gradually damage                             Waterproofing coating and, therefore, lead to premature failure of the whole                             Roofing structure. Multiple and periodic processes of freezing-thawing of water in                             Stagnant zones lead to premature failure of waterproofing                             
This problem is related to the violation or incorrect device of slopes and                             Countercloons on the roof. The "flat" roof is only in theory, in life it is still                             Sloping, only the angle of the slope (slope) in it is minimal - 1.5-4% (2% slope means a rise of 2                             Cm for each meter of length). It is best if the slope is given by a bearing foundation, but                             It is also possible to use traditional methods for the device of the rounder, to                             Example claydite or insulating slabs with a slope.
On flat roofs with                             The main bias of more than 1.5% significantly reduces the risk of possible leaks: decreases                             The number of stagnant zones, the load on the base decreases, and the stability                             Structures to damage by microorganisms, punctures of waterproofing plant roots.                             The appearance of a small defect in the roof covering on the surface with the required gradient is not                             Will lead to serious consequences, while on flat roofs with a slope of less than 1.5%                             Water penetrates into the cracks, exacerbating existing problems.
One option                             Is the device of the main slope of the roof, given by the most load-bearing structure, for example                             Slabs of overlapping. This gradient is set in the process of laying. Steam insulation

                        

Before carrying out measures for the vapor barrier it is necessary to wait until the coupler and the stranger                             Completely dry, otherwise the increased humidity and related problems you get in                             The very beginning of the roofing.
It is necessary to install all engineering equipment,                             Requiring mechanical fastening. For example, to fix to the bearing plates rainwater funnels,                             Compensators for expansion joints, branch pipes for the passage of engineering equipment, anchor                             Bolts.
If on the surface of the screed after the rain there are puddles - this is the first signal that,                             That the measures taken to arrange the deviations were not carried out correctly, the roof should already be                             work. In any case, excess water should be removed (manually with rags or a vacuum cleaner - this is                             Someone as it will), and dry the roof.
Steam insulation is a reinforced                             Fiberglass bitumen-polymer membrane or vapor barrier film, which is laid                             Over the screed, and at the edge of the roof, wind up on a vertical plane to a height greater than                             Height of insulation. The seams are glued with special tape or sealed. Main Function                             Vapor barrier - protection of the insulation against water vapor coming from inside the house, prevention                             Its wetting, bloating and guaranteeing the preservation of all the original properties. This happens                             Because in buildings with a flat roof, as a rule, under the reinforced concrete slab there are always warm                             Premises. That is, under the roof is always a "+", and over the roof a few months in a year                             The temperature is always lower, and with the sign "-".
It goes without saying that if                             The dissonance is carried out with the help of insulation plates, then the vapor barrier is stacked up to them.

Polymer membranes

                        

In addition to bitumen-polymer roll materials, polymer rolls                             Materials, for which the term "roofing membranes" is firmly entrenched. It is worth noting that                             Some other roofing materials are often called membranes. It's connected with                             Borrowing the term from Western technical literature, where all roofing rolls                             Materials are called membranes.
Polymer materials form two main groups,                             Differing in technical and operational characteristics: elastomers and thermoplastics.                             The elastomers used in the manufacture of roofing materials include manufactured                             From EPDM (synthetic rubber - ethylene-propylene-diene-monomer), and to thermoplastics - from                             PVC.
Polymer membranes are characterized by higher strength and durability than                             Bitumen-polymeric materials, elasticity, good resistance to atmospheric phenomena,                             Oxidation and exposure to ultraviolet rays, as well as frost resistance. However, polymeric                             Membranes, as a rule, are 20-30% more expensive than bitumen-polymer materials.
Distinctive                             The feature of polymeric materials is the large width of the membranes. Thanks to this, you can                             Choose the best option for buildings of all sizes and configurations and thereby reduce                             Number of joints and seams.

                        

The most common are three main types of polymer roofing membranes.
-                             EPDM membranes. The seams of membranes can be assembled using a special two-sided                             Self-adhesive tape without heating. The EPDM membrane has a high elasticity, low                             Weight, resistance to sudden changes in temperature. Also produced are reinforced                             EPDM membranes. They are more durable, but less elastic. The main advantage of roofing                             Waterproofing systems is that when repairing the roof EPDM membranes can be stacked                             Over the old ruberoid carpet, which reduces the complexity of the preparatory work.                             Imported EDPM membranes are quite expensive.
- Membranes based on thermoplastic                             Polyolefins (TPO). Stitches in such material are fastened with special welding machines with                             Using hot air. Due to the reinforcing layer (polyester mesh) the material is more                             Resistant to mechanical stress, but less elastic than EPDM membranes. TPO membrane                             It is advisable to use it on new constructions, on roofs of complex configuration and where                             There is a high risk of accidental damage to the membrane (residential buildings, roofs, over which there are                             Floors), as well as in cases where the roof will be subjected to increased mechanical                             Loads during operation and construction.
- PVC-membranes. Sealing of seams                             Is produced by welding with hot air. They have high strength (reinforced with polyester                             Grid) and a variety of colors (9 standard colors, there is a possibility                             Devices of a transparent membrane). Well tolerate roughness and deformation of the substrate. Wide                             The choice of membranes with different characteristics allows them to be used in different conditions.                             PVC membranes are often used in Western countries, they have not yet received                             Due distribution, as their installation requires special equipment.

                        

Seamless roofing materials

Despite the fact that the estimated lifetime of modern roll materials is significantly                             Increased, they still did not lose the main drawbacks - leaks in the joints and                             The need for regular repairs. For the duration of their service, both constructive solutions                             And applied technologies and equipment for gluing roll materials. To avoid                             Leakage at the junctions, seamless materials such as mastics and liquid                             Rubber.
Modern mastics can be used as a roofing material or                             Adhesive compound for the construction of a roofing carpet from roll materials for both erection                             New roofs, and for repairing old ones. Mastic is a liquid-viscous homogeneous                             Mass, which after deposition on the surface and hardening turns into a monolithic                             coating. The composition of the mastic is divided into bitumen, bitumen-polymer and polymer.
                            The composition of the mastic can include a solvent, fillers and various additives, including                             Giving the material the desired color. Bitumen, bitumen-polymer and polymeric mastics                             Differ from similar roll materials in that they form in the waterproofing layer                             Directly on the roof surface. Otherwise they have the same properties.                             Some types of mastic (the so-called cold mastic) in the process of construction                             Works do not require preheating. To improve the strength characteristics                             Mastic roofs, they can be reinforced with glass wool or fiberglass. Reinforcement increases                             Strength, but reduces the elasticity of the mastic layer. Therefore, it can be performed only in                             Separate nodes (usually in junctions and conjugations).
The advantages of mastic roofs                             It is possible to carry absence of places of joints and seams in a roofing carpet. Lack of mastic layer                             Is that it is difficult to achieve a guaranteed thickness of the insulating film, especially when                             Large inclines and uneven surfaces. Therefore, you must either carefully prepare                             Surface, or increase the consumption of material. Both of which lead to an increase in the cost                             Coating.
But to date, developed mastics that allow you to control                             Quality and thickness of the layer, as well as to minimize the consumption of material due to the application                             The original method - applying mastic in two layers. First, the first layer of one                             Color, and then the second - a contrasting color. The thickness of the applied second layer should be                             Be so that the first layer does not shine through.
The liquid rubber that represents                             An emulsion based on oil derivatives with the addition of elastomers of natural rubber,                             Can be used to ensure the waterproofness of the roof of any structure. As a result,                             Applying liquid rubber to the surface of the roof (by spraying with a special                             Equipment) a single seamless polymerized membrane is formed, which is                             Absolutely water-, steam- and gas-tight.
To lacks of liquid rubber it is possible to carry                             That for the normal course of the process of vulcanization of work it is necessary to produce only                             At a temperature of +10 ° С and relative humidity of air no more than 50%. Do not apply                             Coating for materials whose moisture index exceeds 30%. The basis must be                             Dry, clean and smooth.
At a covering from liquid rubber two enemies - the sun and external                             Mechanical effects. Guaranteed service life of UV-free membrane                             Of liquid rubber thickness of 3 mm - 7 years, with luck - 10 years. Would you like to increase this period                             For an unexploited roof? Then the liquid rubber should be protected from ultraviolet radiation.                             The simplest with a reasonable way is to paint with light paint (without solvents) or                             To spray a layer of acrylic-polymeric mastic - in general, any paint or mastic of light                             Tones, which will lie on the rubber. And then the life of the coating will be increased for that period,                              The protective paint remains on the rubber. Will wash off, fall asleep, peel off after 3 years -                              It means that for 3 years the service life of liquid rubber has been extended. Do you want more? Restore after 3                              Year protection.

Aerators

                        

Water vapor, as already mentioned, is a danger to thermal insulation, so from                             Its bases are covered with a layer of vapor barrier. Meanwhile, even a reliable vapor barrier is not                             Guarantees 100% protection from steam, it penetrates through it, but in that amount,                             Which layer of a heater is able to pass through itself.
Further density                             Waterproofing coating does not allow it to evaporate, and it accumulates in the insulation. AT                             As a result, the latter loses thermal insulation properties, and on the ceiling of the premises appear                             Wet spots. In addition, the accumulation of moisture causes swelling of the waterproofing carpet. Water,                             Freezing at negative temperatures and increasing in volume, tears the waterproofing from                             Grounds. Temperature changes and mechanical stresses lead to the formation of cracks, and                             The roof begins to leak. To avoid these troubles, it must be done                             "Breathing". To do this, install aerators - special devices made of metal or                             Plastic pipes of different diameters, covered with caps in the form of umbrellas. Aerators evenly                             Distribute over the entire area of ​​the roof, choosing for them the highest points. These devices                             Work due to the difference in pressure created by airflows, and output excess                             Wet vapors from the under-roof space, preventing the formation of bubbles and flaking                             Roofing material.
