Heating systems: how to choose

Today, for many peoples, it is no longer a secret that gas will gradually rise in price and Soon it will be expensive and not profitable to heat houses with gas boilers. What alternative ways can you bring home to your home? And how costly will these options be?

If the house is heated not with liquefied gas, but with gas from a nearby pipeline, The gas flow rate can be calculated using the following formula:

L = Q /(qН×0,92)

Where L - volumetric fuel consumption per hour, m³

Q - necessary heat output for heating, kW

qН – Net calorific value of fuel

0,92 – Efficiency of the boiler plant

In addition, modern designs of gas boilers are very convenient to use. Gas supply at They occur continuously, so watch out for the flame and constantly ignite the unit Owners do not have to. If it happens that for a while the supply of gas Stops, then thanks to the signal from a special sensor, when the gas arrives, the burner Will light up automatically without human intervention.

Pellet boilers are a convenient solution

After gas boilers pellet - one of the cheapest alternatives in the market of heating Equipment. The cost of granulated pellets and their consumption is less than that of diesel boilers and Other solid fuel, although, of course, the pellet aggregates themselves are more expensive than other solid fuel Analogues. An important positive feature of the unit is that pellet fuel Provides a uniform generation of a given power of thermal energy, while c Coal and firewood power varies cyclically. Power of the boiler equipped with pellet The burner is adjustable in a wide range due to the balancing of the fuel supply and Air.

Servicing pellet boiler is quite simple. You only need to load it yourself into the hopper fuel. Pellets are fed to the furnace from a special hopper by an automatic conveyor. The capacity of the standard bunker provides the boiler with fuel for about 7 days, then the storage Are refilled. If desired, this process can be completely automated. There are systems, In which pellets from the warehouse are fed by a separate conveyor.

Diesel - economical installation

The advantage of diesel heating is the low installation price. Any other kind Heating, based on combustion, will usually require a large installation cost. AT The rest of such devices are 50% more expensive than gas ones, and the efficiency is about 75-85%. Diesel boilers, as a rule, are popular with owners, living areas Which will soon have to go through the gasification process. The fact is that for translation A heating boiler with liquid fuel for gas does not need to produce any complicated operations, Simply change the burner and make a reconfiguration. Installing the system Is carried out very quickly - within a few hours.

Disadvantages of diesel systems are the regular maintenance and the need for observation Behind the system, as well as in the high cost of operation. In addition, diesel fuel itself - pleasure is not cheap. In addition, the installation of a diesel heating system requires Device storage of fuel, and this is also a considerable expense item.

Electricity is an expensive pleasure

Electric boilers are far from the most economical heating equipment in the process However, let's first consider the advantages of electric boilers. First, the equipment itself is cheaper than many other types of heating units, Secondly, its installation in the presence of certain skills can be done with their own hands. In addition, modern electric boilers allow very precise adjustment Temperature even without the installation of climate control systems. As for electric boilers, You can, without hesitation, argue that such a system is characterized by ease of installation and Operation, a high level of automation, ensuring reliable and high-quality work The whole system. Among other things, electric boilers are also distinguished by the highest Coefficient of efficiency - 100%.

But the disadvantages of this version of heating equipment: high cost Electric power, the dependence of stable operation on the availability of current and quality Electrical network.

Heat Pumps

Such aggregates can be either single or auxiliary Source of heat. So, ground heat pumps are a full-fledged alternative to a gas boiler. They work with the same high performance at any time of day and year. Of Disadvantages - a high initial cost, payback more than 10 years, need a large area Ground for installation of a ground collector. In addition, even the best heat pumps work only To a temperature of -25 ° C, with the fall in temperature on the street, their efficiency drops.

A heat pump is a device that converts the low-temperature heat of a source into a High-temperature heat required for heating the room. In this case, the source of heat Can be not only the earth, but also water, air. The most common heat pump Are those that use the energy of the earth. This is due to the best combination of price and Consumer qualities of such devices, efficiency of their application. Efficiency, The truth, depends also on a way of heating of the house, degree of its warmth, tightness Windows, etc. The most rational is the use of low-temperature heating systems, For example, the so-called "warm floors". This is due to the low-temperature mode of heating Water heat pump, which means that traditional radiators will require increased Size.

Of the disadvantages - solar collectors in our temperature conditions can not be Used as a single source of energy, because In winter, the collectors work with Very low efficiency. In addition, the payback period of solar collectors is not yet Inferior to heat pumps - from 7 to 10 years.
