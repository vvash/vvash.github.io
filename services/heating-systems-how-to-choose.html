Heating systems: how to choose

Today the market offers us a lot of modern materials for the device                             Heating system of a private house. Let's look at the most useful ones.

                        
                        

Which boiler should I prefer?

                        

Perhaps the most important component of heating the house is a source of heat, the holy of holies,                             Which converts energy into heat. Based on the type of energy preferred for heating                             Dwellings, and it is worth choosing a cauldron. Technologies do not stand still. Modern producers                             Ready to offer eco-friendly, high-performance and long-life boilers. As a result,                             Gradually in return for morally obsolete tenu installations using electricity,                             Induction and electrode boilers arrive, and the place of gas boilers is occupied by condensing boilers.                             So why pay attention to them when choosing a heating system?

                        

Induction

                        

Converts electricity to heat by electromagnetic induction. In fact - this is a labyrinth                             Pipes with inductor-tip. The principle of the induction boiler is                             Transformation of electric power into an electromagnetic field. It heats the core                             Device, and it in turn transfers excess heat to the heat carrier - water, oil or                             Ethylene glycol. Rapid heating of the liquid provokes convection currents, which direct                             Heated coolant up.

                        

Induction boilers can use any liquid heat transfer media: water, oil or antifreeze.                             This is especially important in abnormally cold winters. Hence, if the power of the pipe is cut off                             Will not burst, and with the restoration of power supply the system will continue to work.

                        

Such a construction is durable, because the boiler does not contain any moving parts that                             Completely eliminates mechanical wear, and microvibration due to magnetic                             Vibrations, gives 100% protection of the core from scale. Induction boilers are conspicuous                             They also exceed the devices with their failing heating elements, and                             Electrode, in which the anode dissolves in a few years.

                        

Sellers position induction boilers as capable of saving electricity by consuming                             At the same heat transfer by 20-30% less analogues. However, do not take this information                             At face value: any direct heating appliance for generating 1 kWh of thermal energy                             Spends 1 kW / h of electricity. The only difference is that the efficiency is not affected by dispersion                             Heat around the boiler, but for this the heat source must be located directly in the                             Heated room. This is easily achieved, because induction boilers are different                             Small size and can be mounted in any room.

                        

Electrode

Also the device is direct action. Heating of the coolant in such a device is achieved by                             Passing an electric current through it. The voltage in the electrodes ionizes                             Coolant, and the ions move chaotically in it, but no electrolysis is observed, because the cathode and                             The anode is swapped with the frequency of the electrical network. Since heat energy is generated                             Directly in the coolant, i.e. The heat carrier is also a heating medium                             Element, the electrode boiler can save up to 40% of electricity compared to                             Teen.

                        

Electrode boilers are equipped with automatic control, which operates on request. They are missing                             Heating element, and therefore there is no uncontrolled inertia of heating. As a result,                             The boiler will produce a heat quantity accurately calibrated to the wattage, which is necessary for the heated                             Room. Automation regulates the disconnection of the system from the mains in case of a short circuit,                             Leakage of coolant and excess of the set temperature. Manage this heating system                             You can even not be at home - via the Internet or by phone. Thanks to satellite communication                             The boiler can be "called" and "asked" to warm up the room to the desired temperature.                             Irreplaceable function for owners of country cottages.

                        

An essential disadvantage of the electrode boiler is the need for an autonomous connection                             Power, because when you turn on the device requires a large power of electricity and provokes                             Voltage jump.

                        

Ordinary water can not be a heat carrier in the electrode boiler. We need either substantial                             Preparation of liquid, or use of a special antifreeze intended for systems                             Of this type.

                        

Electrode boilers, like induction boilers, favorably distinguish compactness and quiet operation.                             In addition, they can be mounted in an existing system. However, the prices for the devices themselves and                             The electricity they consume is quite high.

                        

Condensation

                        

Its efficiency is more than 100%. How is this possible, because it contradicts the laws of physics? In addition to                             Heat from the combustion of gas boiler uses the heat of condensation of water vapor. He is capable                             Select the energy of the condensate from the combustion products and thereby save fuel. If in                             Classical gas boilers, the water evolved in the process of burning fuel turns into steam                             And carried out through the chimney, then in condensation this steam is collected in a special                             Heat exchanger. The heat thus obtained is then used to heat                             Coolant, which significantly increases the efficiency of the installation, bringing it to 107-111% in relation to                             Gas boilers that are not capable of recovering heat from the condensate.

For greater savings, it is recommended to connect the boiler to the outdoor temperature sensor. it                             Will make the return water temperature in the heating system below the dew point (about                             50 ° C). When included in a low-temperature system, condensing boilers can reduce                             Gas consumption up to 35% per year and, accordingly, to cut gas costs by 35%. This is quite                             Compensates for the higher cost of boilers compared to traditional systems. Low                             The operating temperature makes condensing boilers ideal for a two-circuit system                             Heating.

                        

Using condensation to obtain additional heat also significantly increases                             Service life of the boiler. After all, condensation from conventional boilers in the chimney and hit                             Moisture back to the structure causes corrosive wear and deterioration of gas combustion. Details                             Condensing heat exchanger are made of materials resistant to chemical corrosion,                             Such as stainless steel and silumin. This makes it possible to extend the service life in half by                             Compared to conventional gas boilers.

                        

For the operation of the condensing boiler, a sealed chimney with forced                             Extract, resistant to acids. Air and gas are mixed before entering the burner,                             Due to this and special burner technology, the emission of harmful substances with combustion products                             Decreases by 80%. Such a high level of ecological compatibility of condensing boilers was                             The reason for the prohibition in Europe of the use of gas devices at the legislative level.

                        

Processes in the system are controlled by automation. On the one hand, it allows more accurate                             Control switching on and off of the boiler, thereby saving fuel; on the other hand, when                             Power failure, the boiler will automatically turn off, stopping the burner and                             Leaving the house without heating.

                        

Radiator or convector?

After installing the boiler it is worth taking care of the heat exchangers, which will deliver heat to each                             Corner of the house. They can be divided into two large groups: radiators and convectors. So                             It turned out that the radiators had taken root in the post-Soviet space. They work on                             Analogy with the sun: up to 80% of the heat is transmitted by radiation in the infrared spectrum and provides                             Constant temperature in the room. Convectors also form circular flows in the room                             Warm air. A heating convector consisting of a number of plates placed on                             A pipe through which air flows, heats the cold air between these plates. Warm air,                             As is known, is easier, and it tends upward, freeing up space between the plates for a new portion                             Cold air. So, on which heat exchanger to stop?

                        

Of radiators for an autonomous heating system, the most popular                             Aluminum options. This is the most inexpensive option with high heat output (210 W per section                             At a standard height), because the metal heats up quickly and gives off heat as quickly                             Room - for warming up to the desired temperature, it takes 10-12 minutes. Operating pressure                             The heat exchanger is 16 atmospheres. The low weight of this device facilitates transportation                             And installation. When using a radiator for heating the room at 10 m? You need only 2 liters of water. Behind                             The account of a fast warming up of a radiator installation on it of the thermal valve can save up to 30%                             Energy.

                        

But there are aluminum batteries and flaws. These include the instability to corrosion due to                             High metal activity and a relatively short service life. Operational period                             Such heat exchangers do not exceed 15 years. To prolong the life of such batteries, you can                             Use a gentle heat carrier (pH 7-8), necessary to maintain the protective film on                             The inner surface of the product. When choosing an aluminum radiator, consider:                             Prefabricated models are not strictly suitable for heating systems that use                             Antifreeze or any other chemical compound, because they contain rubber                             Seals.

                        

In fact, aluminum radiators from different manufacturers differ only in design -                             The qualitative characteristics are approximately identical. However, if you see a model, significantly                             Second in price among analogues, do not rush to run with it to the cashier. Low price differ                             Models from secondary aluminum. The price, perhaps, is the only advantage of such batteries.                             Aluminum radiators from recycled materials are less reliable than their counterparts made of                             For the first time the processed material.

Increased strength in comparison with aluminum batteries are bimetallic                             Radiators. These are the same aluminum heat exchangers, but inside of them is installed a steel                             The collector, which moves hot water. Accordingly, such a design is more                             Resistant to corrosion due to steel, which prolongs the service life and increases the resistance                             Pressure up to 40 atm. At the same time, it retains thermal conductivity akin to aluminum                             Analogue. Naturally, the price of a bimetallic radiator is higher.

                        

Fans of natural air circulation in the premises should consider the installation option                             Convector. Copper-aluminum convectors are the most resistant to competition in the market.                             Thanks to copper, the service life of such devices is 50-60 years. The construction of convectors in the root                             Differs from the design of radiators. If in the radiator batteries the coolant fills                             All the device, then in the convector - only a tube with fins. This allows you to use the whole                             From 0.4 to 0.8 liters of coolant. Coupled with high thermal conductivity of aluminum and compact                             By placing plates this allows to increase the efficiency by more than 50% in comparison with radiators.                             But in every barrel of honey there is a fly in the ointment. Convector due to its design features                             Collects dust, and then chases it through the room along with circulating air streams.

                        

Another plus of the convector is that the heating element does not come in contact directly with the surface.                             Therefore, children will not be able to burn themselves on the convector body. In this case, even at low                             Temperature of the coolant, the room temperature will remain comfortable.

                        

The convector will be an indispensable solution for a room with a large glazing surface or                             Places with high humidity, such as a winter garden, a greenhouse or a swimming pool.

                        

Unfortunately, copper-aluminum convectors - a pleasure quite expensive. Expensiveness                             Is caused by the use of expensive thermoregulatory elements. The price also increases the availability of                             Decorative screen of wood, stone or stainless steel. So such a "battery" can be                             Not only as a heating appliance, but also as a piece of furniture that does not need                             In disguise. You can add another 1 point for aesthetic functionality: installation of a convector                             Leaves room for special architectural solutions. So, the heater can be                             Place on the wall, on the floor and even put it in the floor.

                        

The main thing - do not lose with the pipes

Bonding the boiler and the heat exchanger is perhaps the most important stage of installing the heating system                             System. It is very important to choose the right materials for pipes, because if the radiators,                             Convectors or even a boiler, if desired, can be replaced without serious consequences, then a replacement                             Pipes, often built into walls or hidden under the floor, will require a cardinal                             Reconstruction of the home.

                        

The most common type of pipes in heating systems is polypropylene. The main                             Way, because of its cheapness and reliability of connections. Pipes made of polypropylene more than                             2 times cheaper than metal-plastic analogs, especially due to connecting parts. At that time                             The time is just their connection is famous for its durability and ease of installation. Thermal                             Welding, easily and quickly produced by a conventional soldering iron (as you see, special skills are not                             Required), allows you to create a monolithic connection, identical in structure to the pipe.

                        

To work with metal plastic, expensive tools or physical strength will be needed. But                             The connection will remain remote from the monolith. Nevertheless, the metal-plastic can be bent, and                             To create a corner when installing polypropylene pipes, you will have to use the corners and                             Tees. Polypropylene pipe has a high mechanical strength, so that if                             Editing accidentally hit it or drop something heavy on it, big trouble will not happen.

                        

The guaranteed lifetime of polypropylene is 25 years. Reinforced polypropylene pipes have                             Operating temperature + 95 ° C, respectively, they can be safely used in heating systems.                             Another advantage of polypropylene is its resistance to thawing. That is, if the water in the pipe                             It is frozen, you can not worry about its integrity, it will expand and with thawing will take                             The original form.

                        

The cross-linked polyethylene will be convenient in the application for radial wiring in a screed or the installation of a warm                             Sex. The material got its name thanks to the structure. The links of ethylene molecules, as it were, are transverse                             Are sewn into a three-dimensional grid. "Entanglement" of molecules allows polyethylene to remain strong                             On the fracture and resist cracking, which means it's easy to bend. Also such pipes                             Have a molecular memory.

                        

Ensuring the thermal stability of pressurized material is the main objective                             Cross-linked polyethylene. Plain polyethylene is not suitable for heating pipes, because it                             Deforms at high temperatures, while after cross-linking, the recommended                             Material temperature + 95 ° C.

                        

It should be taken into account that cross-linked polyethylene is a material for laying hidden engineering                             Communications. It can not be used in places where there is sunlight; he                             Is destroyed by UV radiation.

Innovative solutions in heating systems

                        

We reviewed the most popular and winning materials. But, anyway, they are all -                             Only modern ways to implement projects, invented dozens and even hundreds of years ago.                             Now the market presents cardinally new solutions that are designed to significantly reduce                             Costs and negative impact on the environment.

                        

Warm floor

                        

A warm floor will help reduce heat loss through the roof. Just imagine: with heating                             Radiators heat in the room is distributed unevenly. As you know, warm air                             Goes up, which means that if at the height of a person's growth there will be 20 ° C, comfortable for life, then under                             Ceiling air will warm up to not less than 25 ° C. And the amount of heat leaving through the roof                             And overlap, depends just on the temperature directly below them. If you turn                             Floor in the heat source, then when it is heated to 25 ° C, at a height of 2 m the temperature will be                             23 ° C, and under the ceiling and not more than 20 ° C. So heat loss will be greatly reduced, and                             The temperature regime will be even higher than from the usual heating radiators.

                        

Now there are three technologies for warm floor: water, realized with the help of a heating cable                             And electric. The first option will be convenient in case of water heating: in the screed                             A pipe is laid without connections, the temperature of the coolant in which can not exceed                             40 ° C. Heating cable will be an alternative to the water floor, if the energy of the heating system in                             The house is recycled from electricity. Such a cable can be laid on the substrate or                             Fed in the form of heating mats. The film electric floor can be mounted under                             Laminate, linoleum or tile, because it is a thin dielectric substrate with                             Applied by current-carrying channels.

                        

Heat storage batteries

                        

Using a heat accumulator, you can save considerably by using for heating                             Cooler nightly electricity tariff. The heat accumulator is                             A heat-insulated container of large volume that retains the heated coolant and                             Gradually gives it to heating appliances. For owners of solid fuel boilers, this is                             The device is especially useful - the collected heat is enough to heat the boiler no more than once in                             Day.

                        

Solar collectors

                        

Independent energy supply is not yet widespread in our country. Although both                             An additional source of saving resources this type of obtaining thermal energy is quite                             Tempting. The primitive solar collector is a tank of black color, covered with glass                             Construction. The sun's rays heat the container, and the glass reduces by convection loss                             Heat. Recently, the most common collectors on vacuum tubes. Principle                             The action of such a tube is similar to that of a thermos. Such solar cells are converted into                             Heat 94% of the sun's rays.

The collector is not able to provide the house with round-the-clock heating, as there is no sun at night, and in                             The cloudy weather reduces heat production, but as a source of additional savings                             May well be considered. In addition, with gas heating, you can use                             Solar collector to generate electricity - the house of the solar battery is quite enough.                             By the way, the surplus of electricity received from alternative sources in Belarus can be                             Surrender to the state at a triple tariff!

                        

Heat Pumps

                        

The heat pump does not depend on the sun, but also does not offer free heating. Furthermore,                             So far, the prices for operating costs are even higher than if using a backbone                             gas. The main trump card of this system is independence from non-renewable hydrocarbons. By investing                             Funds in the heat pump, you can not be afraid of rising gas prices.

                        

The pump uses an average kilowatt of electricity, but not for the production of heat, but for                             Pumping 3-6 kW of heat energy into a housing from another source. Principle of operation of the device                             Something like a refrigerator, but more often it works for heating. Although heat pumps with                             The same success can both warm the house in the winter, and cool it in the summer heat.

                        

The source of thermal energy can be ground. Pumps using geothermal heat,                             Are in the upper price segment. At the same time this type of heating is the most reliable and                             Independent of the climate. Expensive, and the device itself, and installation. After all, the probe-heat exchanger                             It is necessary to place in the wells drilled for several tens of meters. The temperature there is not                             Depends on the climate on the surface. Of course, you can place the device horizontally                             (Below freezing point), so that the soil temperature is 10-12 ° C, but this requires                             The surface is several times larger than the area of ​​the house. And use it as                             Homestead is no longer possible: the roots of plants will freeze, because                             The collector cools the soil very much.

                        

The heat exchanger can also be placed in water. This requires either an ice-free                             Convenient solution, because to obtain an inexhaustible source of heat, it is enough to place in                             Water heat exchanger and sink it with goods), or a high level of groundwater in                             Site. In this case, the pump is mounted as follows: two wells are drilled, one of them                             A pump is installed to draw water, this water passes through the heat exchanger and, giving                             Heat, is returned through a second well.

                        

The closest to the reality of the price of heat pumps are "air-to-air". These protozoa                             Devices, in fact, inverter air conditioners, complete with a heating cable for                             Perimeter of the outdoor unit, which prevents frost. The installation instructions are the same as                             For a conventional air conditioner. But these pumps can be used all year round only in warm                             Climatic zone, because at a temperature well below zero, they significantly reduce                             Efficiency, and at -30 ° C and are not at all able to work.
