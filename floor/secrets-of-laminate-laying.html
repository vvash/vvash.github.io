Secrets of laminate laying

Laminate is not placed in rooms with high humidity: saunas, showers, bathrooms Rooms. Before the beginning of packing, the packed laminate is allowed to stand 48 hours at t + 18 ° and Humidity & gt; 70%.

Ways of styling:

  • Using glue
  • Using your profile
  • Using driving locks

Instruments:

  • A hammer
  • Electric jigsaw (or saw)
  • Wooden strip 15-20 cm
  • Pencil
  • Roulette

Surface preparation

Laminate can be laid over fiberboard, particle board, linoleum.

That when walking there was no sonorous noise, and the seams did not disperse, the ground should be level. At small differences, the screed is poured with a self-leveling mass. With significant The roughness begins with a cement screed, after which they move to a self-leveling device.

Substrate laying

After the substrate dries, the substrate is laid. Substrate - heat- And a soundproof material, which is needed to separate the rough floor base from the Laminate, protect from moisture.

Substrate:

  • Insulation boards
  • Synthetic substrate with a metal film
  • Technical plug
  • Cardboard made of woolen fiber

Laminate laying

Roll rolls of polyethylene perpendicular to our future laying of the laminate overlapping by 15-20 cm, do not lay in prityk to the walls, but 4-7 cm more. You can fix the film between each other with adhesive tape or adhesive tape. Further spread the substrate under the laminate, here it should be laid flat, butt in the joint.

And so, the surface is prepared, we proceed to laying the laminate.

The direction should be selected parallel to the sun's rays. In long rooms, such as a corridor, the direction is chosen perpendicular to the long side, regardless of the incidence of light.

The laminated board can be connected together by means of glue or on the lock joints. Types of lock latches are different, they affect the fastening of panels to each other.

My advice to you is: use a laminate with snaps. In case of damage to one of the boards, it is easy to disassemble and without damage to the whole floor to make a replacement.

The first panel we begin to stack from the far left corner, if you look from the entrance. Locks for the entire first row saw off with a hacksaw, jigsaw or electric jigsaw, if any.

Between the wall and the board it is necessary to insert spacers with a thickness of 8-10 mm, after the final laying of the laminate they must be removed. Thanks to the spacers, cracks remain, in case the laminate widens a little over time. If you do not take this into account, the floor may eventually buckle.

Take the first tile of the laminate and to it from the front side we place the second at an angle of 30-45 degrees and click it with the click down. In the same spirit, we spread the first row. If the latter does not fit, but it rarely suits, we saw off and use the remainder in the further packing. Do not forget to leave room for the braces. For laying the second row, we need to cut into two equal parts a laminate board, a kind of brickwork is obtained, it will give strength to the whole floor. By the same principle, we stack all subsequent series.

There are a lot of hard-to-reach places: near walls, doors, under batteries. In such places, sometimes it is not possible to join at an angle, in this case we join it in a plane near a hard-to-reach place and hammer it with a hammer through a wooden bar or special device.

Another scheme of laying: you can lay a whole series and subsequently fix it to the previous one.

When laying the last row, we use a special clamp, with its help.

If you are going to lay glue laminate, then the principle of operation is almost the same. Putting this method, you get a more waterproof surface, but to disassemble such a floor without damage, alas, will not work.

After completing the installation, remove the spacers and fix the plinth. When docking with other floor coverings or door thresholds, the laminate is joined using a special metal or plastic profile.
